How It Works

Three steps to a guaranteed profit

AlgoForAll ArbEdge does the math — you just place the bets.

Click "Fetch & Scan" in the Player Props tab. Live odds are pulled from 30+ books via The Odds API on demand — no wasted API credits.

Our engine groups every player prop across all books, finds Over/Under pairs where the combined implied probability falls below 100%, and filters out noise (<1% and >10% profits).
